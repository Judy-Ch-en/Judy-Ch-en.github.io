Something useful about English —Unit3

New words and expressions(a-z)

A

  • advice on sth

  • akward adj. 局促不安的；令人尴尬的；难对付的；笨拙的

    –suggestion(s) for/on about sth

  • at a loss 无语；困惑；不确定

  • apologize v.
    –apologize to sb for (doing) sth
    –apology n.
    make an apology

B

  • bring out 取出；出版；生产

    --bring out the best in a person

  • basis n. 基础;原因;基准（basis-bases）

    form the basis of

  • benefit n.(c)

             vt. 使受益

        vi. 得益于

C

  • count on sb=rely/depend on sb

  • come and go

  • contact v.

              n. contact with sb/contact between Aand B

  • come between A and B 损害AB间的关系，离间；妨碍

  • comfort v. 宽慰，抚慰

               n.

  • company n.

  • crowd n.

             v.

D

  • definitely adv.

E

  • explode vi./vt.(使爆发)

    –explode with sth(anger/laughter)

  • eat away at sth 腐蚀

  • efficient adj.

  • extra adj.

          adv. 额外，另外；特别，格外

  • escape v.(避开，避免；被遗忘)

             n.

F

  • frank adj.
  • face to face(face-to-face)
  • form the basis of
  • failure n. (未履行；故障)

G

  • get along with sb
    =get on well with sb
  • get over 克服，恢复常态（ the difficulties）

H

  • hang out 闲逛；厮混（v.悬挂 hang-hung-hung;绞刑 hang-hanged-hanged）
    –hang around with sb
    –hang up 挂断电话；悬挂；结束
    –hang on 别挂电话；坚持；等一会儿
    –hang on to sth 紧紧抓住；保留；保存

  • horrible adj.令人震惊的；可恶的；极坏的

  • high(low) point=climax

  • hand sth over
    –hand out

I

  • in the wrong 有错；应承担责任

  • in any case 无论如何，总之
    –in this/that case 如果这样/那样
    –in no case 绝不=at no time/by no means 倒装
    –in case (that)
    in case of (doing) sth

  • in person 亲身，亲自

  • imagine doing something

  • ignore v.

    –ignorance n.无知

  • in full measure

J

  • judge v.评价（尤指批评）；判断，认为
    –judge from/by
    judge sb on sth
    –judgement n.

           n. 法官；裁判员

L

  • let go of sth.
  • long to do sth/for sth

M

  • make it 能够出席；准时到达；获得成功；幸免于难
    –make up 组成；化妆；编造；（with） 和解；（for）弥补
    –make for 促成

A happy family make for a happy child.

  • medium n. 传播信息的媒介，方法；手段，工具
  •             adj. 中等的；中号的

   --make (both) ends meet 收支平衡；量入为出

  • measure vt. 估量，判定；测量

                 n. 措施；衡量

    –measure sth by sth

  • misunderstand v. 误解；误会 （misunderstood-misunderstood）

  • be meant to do sth 注定做；应做

    – sb mean to do sth

  • more often than not=generally speaking/sometimes

O

  • on the rocks （关系）陷于困境，濒临崩溃
    =in difficulty

  • original adj. 起初的；独创的；原作的

  • opinion n. 意见，看法；（群体的）观点，信仰

    in one’s opinion=as far as sb is concerned/from one’s point of view

    opinion of/about

  • openness n.

  • one-sided

P

  • pick up the pieces 收拾碎片
    收拾残局；努力恢复正常

She broke my heart,and I’m still picking up the pieces.

  • point out
  • poet n. 诗人

Q

  • quality n. 品德，素质；质量；特征

              adj. 优质的，高品质的

R

  • recover vi. 健康恢复；恢复常态

    –recover from sth

  • rethink v.

               vt. 全额收回；寻回

  • respond to sb/sth

    –respond to one’s messages=reply to sb

    –response n.

  • raft n. 木筏，筏（c）

  • reflection n.

  • recognize v. （承认；意识到）

    –recognize as=consider as/think of as/refer to as

    –It is recognized that… 承认是

    –recognition n. 认出，承认

  • respect v.

             n.（方面）show respect for/to
         
                            in some/many respects

S

  • sight n.视野；视力；看见
    –be in/out of sight=be (no longer) visible
    –set one’s sights on doing sth =be determined to do/make up one’s mind to do/decide to do决心做某事
    –catch sight of
    –at first sight
    –lose sight of 看不见；忽略=ignore

  • shallow adj. 肤浅的

  • selfless adj.

  • suffer vi. 受苦，受折磨，变差

           vt. 遭受，蒙受

  • slave n. 奴隶

  • seek v.(seek-sought-sought)

  • smooth out 消除问题，克服困难

T

  • through thick and thin 不顾艰难险阻，同甘共苦

  • -trick v. 欺骗，欺诈

         n. 诡计，花招，戏法

    –tricky adj.棘手的; 狡猾的; 难办的; 难对付的; 诡计多端的

  • theme n. 主题，主旋律

  • take on 呈现；具有

    –take off

    –take up

    –take in 吸收，领会

    –take over

  • thorough adj. 彻底的，全面的；仔细的

  • talk one’s problems with sb

U

  • under a lot of stress
  • unlikely pairs

W

  • worth adj./n.
    –worthy adj.
    be worthy to be done/of being done/of sth
    –worthwhile adj. =important/enjoyable/interesting/高尚的
    It’s worthwhile doing/to do sth.=It’s worth doing sth
  • well-meaning adj. 出于好心的；善意的

Grammar rules–定语从句

1.定义

修饰，说明n./pron. 品质与特征
