Discuss an accomplishment,event,or realization that sparked a period of personal growth and a new understanding of yourself or others?

*Discuss an accomplishment,event,or realization that sparked a period of personal growth and a new understanding of yourself or others? (Maximum 350 words) *

 I used to be a so-called social phobia in primary school. Whenever I was making a presentation in public, I felt deadly nervous. However, my attitude towards public speaking completely changed in 8th grade.

  In May,2021, I was instructed by my English teacher to join in an English speaking competition in Jiangsu province. I felt as if stress was in the air. Fearing that I might let my teacher down, I practiced hard with two other students.

  In the beginning, things were in a complete mass. I didn’t know what to say and how to organize my opinions. Improving the quantity of my public speaking experience was in real earnest. My roommates heard about my problems and offered film my trials. With the assistance of her and the reflections on myself, my speaking standard improved.

  Then, the main target was to increase the quality of my ideas and speech. During this period, I came across different topics and had mind storms with my teammates about how to acquire arguments from various perspectives as well as how to use our arguments to support our opinions. In this way our ability to give response to an unfamiliar topic in a short time interval grew.  I also encouraged my teammate to express her ideas and take on the courage to speak.

  Finally, it was time for presentation. On the stage, holding the microphone in hand, the nerve and stress that had been familiar to me no longer got in the way. The only thing I felt was the excitement of delivering my thoughts to others and the confidence of myself. At last, I got the first prize in the competition. The skills I gained while improving my public speaking standard provided me with a concrete foundation in my later life.

  Without this event, I would perhaps never discover my talent in public speaking and would not then enter Nanjing Foreign Language School. Also, without the event I would never know that only with efforts to step outside of my comfort zone, can I realize my inner potential.
